About Makhosi

A practice built on listening, lineage, and respect.

For over two decades, Makhosi Shabangu has guided individuals and families through consultation, healing, and ceremony — drawing on knowledge received from her own ancestors and elders. Every consultation is approached with discretion, patience, and full attention to the person in front of her.

Discretion

Every consultation is private. What is shared in the room stays in the room.

Lineage

Methods passed down through training and ancestral guidance, not improvised.

Care

Remedies are hand-prepared in small batches, never mass-produced.
